## Product Knowledge

**What is a Tox Tray?**
A tox tray is a specialized organizer tray used by aesthetic injectors (nurses, PAs, physicians) during Botox and neurotoxin injection procedures. It holds vials, syringes, alcohol swabs, gauze, and other supplies in an organized layout during treatment.

**Why ours is different:**
- 3D printed (custom designs, rapid iteration)
- Designed by an actual medspa owner/injector who uses them daily
- Practical layouts based on real workflow needs
- Available in multiple colors and configurations

**Target customer:**
- Aesthetic injectors (RNs, NPs, PAs, MDs)
- Medspa owners outfitting treatment rooms
- Aesthetic training programs and schools
- New injectors setting up their first practice

**Value props:**
- Keeps workspace organized during procedures
- Reduces cross-contamination risk
- Professional look for treatment rooms
- Durable, easy to clean
- Made in the USA

# Aesthetic Record: Tips & Tricks — Check-In to Charting

**For front desk + clinical staff at a weight loss / aesthetics / BHRT practice.**

---

## 1. Check-In

- **Patient Portal pre-work**: Send intake forms, consents, and medical history questionnaires via the patient portal BEFORE the visit. Patients complete on their phone. Staff gets pre-filled charts.
- **Check-in button**: Click "Patient Check In" on the appointment → enter room number → add notes for the provider → "Complete Check In." Patient status updates live on the schedule.
- **Lock Mode (iPad)**: Hand the iPad to the patient for consent signing. Tap the **lock icon** (top right) — this locks AR so they can't see other patient data. Unlock with your AR password or a 4-digit passcode (set up in Settings > Your Profile).
- **Passcode tip**: Every provider should set a personal unlock passcode. Faster than typing your email/password every time you get the iPad back.

## 2. Scheduling

- **Color indicators**: Red calendar icon = patient enrolled in cancellation policy. Green credit card = deposit paid. Use these at a glance.
- **Waitlist**: Add patients with their preferred service, provider, day, and time. Capture a credit card on file so you can book instantly when a slot opens.
- **Schedule templates**: Settings > Schedule Templates. Build blocks for your common days (e.g., "Weight Loss Tuesday" = all GLP-1 follow-ups, "Injection Friday" = neurotoxin + filler). Saves rebuilding weekly.
- **Online booking**: Patients self-book from your website or patient portal. Automates reminders via email/SMS to reduce no-shows.

## 3. Charting Workflow

- **Start a procedure**: Tap "+" on the patient profile → select Cosmetic or Health procedure → name it → attach the appointment.
- **Chart-to-Cart**: Whatever you chart auto-populates to the patient's checkout cart. No handoff needed between provider and front desk. What is charted = what is charged.
- **Injection markings**: Double-tap the face diagram to place a marker. Single-tap to adjust units (+/-). Hold and drag to move placement. Use the orientation tool to rotate needle angle.
- **Bulk select injections**: Hold finger on empty space and drag diagonally to select multiple markings at once. Edit them as a group.
- **Draw tool**: Tap DRAW (top left) for lines, arrows, shapes, text. Use for cannula entry points, directional passes, treatment zones.
- **Traceability**: After marking injections, tap "+ Traceability" → select lot number/batch ID → enter units. This auto-deducts inventory. Do this during the visit — the 48-hour rule flags late entries and can mess with CTC reporting.
- **Photos**: Take before/after photos directly in the procedure. AR has SmartMatch for consistent alignment. Photos attach to the procedure record, not floating in your camera roll.
- **ChartSmart AI** ($30/mo add-on): Dictate or record ambient audio during the procedure. AI generates a complete note. Trained on aesthetic workflows. Can cut charting time ~80%. Worth it if you're behind on notes.

## 4. Dot Phrases

**What they are**: Pre-written note templates you trigger by typing a period (`.`) in any notes field.

**How to create**:
1. Web version: `app.aestheticrecord.com` → Settings ⚙️ → Clinical Documentation → Dot Phrases → Add
2. Enter the trigger name (e.g., `botox`) and the note text
3. Save. Now available in the app.

**How to use**:
1. Open a procedure → tap +ADD NOTES
2. Type `.` — your dot phrase list appears
3. Start typing the name to filter (e.g., `.glp` shows GLP-1 phrases)
4. Select → pre-written note populates → edit as needed → Save

**Must create on web first.** They won't appear on iPad/iPhone until saved on the web version.

## 5. Billing & Checkout

- **Chart-to-Cart**: When charting is done, the front desk sees everything in the cart. No verbal handoff needed.
- **Memberships**: Settings > Memberships. Set tier name, monthly fee, included services, and wallet credits. Auto-bills monthly. Patients get notified on declined cards and can update payment in their portal.
- **Membership hold**: You can pause a membership without canceling. Move billing dates to match patient preference.
- **Packages**: Track remaining sessions. System deducts as used.

## 6. Inventory

- **Auto-deduction**: When you add traceability info to a procedure (lot # + units), inventory deducts automatically.
- **Par levels**: Set minimum stock levels in Inventory settings. Get alerts before you run out.
- **Purchase orders**: Create and send POs to suppliers directly from AR.
- **Lot tracking**: Toggle ON "Lot number and Expiration" in Inventory settings. Required for traceability to work.

## 7. Time-Saving Shortcuts

- **Dot phrases for everything**: Make one for every service you do regularly. 30 minutes of setup saves hundreds of hours.
- **Provider templates** (Q&A format): Standardize charting across providers. Great for multi-provider practices.
- **Medical history pull**: In notes, you can pull answers from the patient's intake forms (allergies, current meds) directly into the chart.
- **Consent expiration**: Set document expiration periods so consents auto-flag when they need renewal. Settings > Document Expiration.
- **Formatting in notes**: Use `#` for headers, `###` for subheaders, `-` for bullets, `**bold**`, `*italic*`. Makes notes scannable.
- **Undo/redo**: Arrows at bottom of the injection screen. Use liberally.
- **Pre/post instructions**: Build once in Settings > Pre & Post Treatment Instructions. Auto-deliver to patients by service type.

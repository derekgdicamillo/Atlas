# BHRT (Bioidentical Hormone Replacement Therapy)

Clinical knowledge base for hormone optimization protocols.

## Content to populate

### Men's Health
- Testosterone replacement: cypionate dosing, injection frequency optimization (weekly vs biweekly vs daily micro-dose)
- Estradiol management: anastrozole dosing, when to use vs when to avoid AI
- HCG protocols: fertility preservation, testicular atrophy prevention
- DHEA supplementation
- Lab panels: total T, free T, SHBG, E2 (sensitive), PSA, CBC (polycythemia monitoring), lipids, metabolic panel
- Monitoring schedules (baseline, 6-week, 12-week, quarterly)
- Side effect management: polycythemia, acne, mood changes, sleep apnea screening

### Women's Health

- Progesterone (oral micronized, topical)
- Testosterone for women (low-dose, sublingual/topical)
- DHEA protocols
- Thyroid optimization (full panel: TSH, free T3, free T4, reverse T3, TPO/TG antibodies)
- Adrenal support and cortisol management
- Perimenopause vs menopause protocol differences
- Lab panels and monitoring schedules
- Contraindications: hormone-receptor positive cancer history, active thromboembolic disease

## Case 1 — "Refractory IBS in a 38-Year-Old Office Worker"

### Intake highlights
- 38F, healthy weight, no major comorbidities
- 4-year history of IBS-M, bloating, alternating constipation/diarrhea, brain fog, eczema flares
- Failed FODMAP, peppermint oil, multiple courses of probiotics
- Travel: Mexico 5 yrs ago with brief diarrhea; Costa Rica 2 yrs ago
- Pets: 2 cats, indoor/outdoor
- Diet: standard, regular sushi
- Labs: GI-MAP — Blastocystis hominis subtype 3 positive, low secretory IgA, dysbiosis, calprotectin mild elevation
- Stool O&P x3: negative
- CBC normal, eosinophils 320, IgE 180

### Tier
2 — symptomatic, immunocompetent, single confirmed organism + dysbiosis

### Decision
Confirmed Blastocystis pathway (decision tree §3.3) + secondary dysbiosis address + Phase 1 prep + microbiome restoration.

### Protocol

**Phase 1 (weeks 1-3)**
- TUDCA 250 mg with each meal
- Triphala 1 g at bedtime
- Magnesium glycinate 400 mg PM
- LMNT electrolytes 1-2 daily
- Bitter tincture pre-meals
- Quercetin 500 mg BID for skin
- Diet shift to anti-parasite plan
- Sauna 3x/week, dry brushing daily

**Phase 2 (weeks 4-9)**
- Tinidazole 2 g daily x 3 d at week 4 (full moon week)
- Tinidazole 2 g daily x 3 d at week 7 (next full moon)
- Berberine 500 mg BID continuous weeks 4-9
- Oregano oil 200 mg BID weeks 4-7
- Bismuth subcitrate 240 mg BID weeks 4-7
- Mimosa Pudica per loading schedule, 4 caps BID by week 4, continued through Phase 2
- S. boulardii 10 billion BID

**Phase 3 (concurrent + 2 wks)**
- BioActive Carbon Foundation 2 caps 1 hr after Tinidazole and herb doses
- Activated charcoal 1 g + bentonite 1 tbsp at bedtime
- Magnesium support continued

**Phase 4 (weeks 10-18)**
- L-glutamine 10 g/day
- Zinc carnosine 75 mg BID x 8 wks
- Bovine colostrum 6 g/day
- Probiotic rotation: S. boulardii continued + L. rhamnosus GG + Bifido infantis + MegaSpore alternating monthly
- PHGG 5 g/day building to 10 g
- Bone broth daily
- Reintroduce fermented foods week 12
- Slowly reintroduce eliminated foods week 14

### Monitoring
- Weekly symptom log
- Stool re-test week 12
- LFT week 6 if any concern
- 3-month post-test for durability

### Stop conditions standard

## Case 2 — "Pediatric Pinworm with Disrupted Sleep"

### Intake highlights
- 7-year-old male, 24 kg
- 6 weeks of nighttime perianal itching, irritability, tooth grinding
- Mom and 4-year-old sister also itching anally
- 2 cats, deworming overdue
- No travel
- No labs needed; classic pinworm

### Tier
1-2 — pediatric pinworm, household coordination

### Decision
Empiric pinworm pathway (decision tree §3.1) + whole household + pets.

### Protocol

**Same day, all household members ≥2 yrs**
- Pyrantel pamoate 11 mg/kg single dose (mom, dad, both kids per weight)
- Repeat exact same dose in 14 days

**Pets**
- Schedule vet deworming this week (pyrantel + praziquantel combo)

**Household decontamination**
- Wash all bedding, sheets, towels, pajamas in hot water today
- Vacuum all bedrooms thoroughly
- Replace toothbrushes
- Trim and scrub all fingernails
- Cotton tight-fitting underwear at night for 2 weeks
- Morning showers (don't bathe) for 2 weeks
- Daily handwashing reinforcement
- Wipe high-touch surfaces

**Pediatric herbal adjunct (4-week course)**
- Mimosa Pudica seed capsules opened in applesauce, ½ adult dose for children = 2 caps daily total
- Garlic in food daily (sneak into pasta sauce, soups)
- 1 tbsp ground pumpkin seed in smoothie/yogurt daily
- Bone broth daily
- S. boulardii 5 billion daily
- Skip wormwood (under age 8 not appropriate)
- Triphala ½ tsp at bedtime if constipated

**Family lifestyle**
- Less sugar, more whole foods for 4 weeks
- Sleep priority — itching disrupting sleep should resolve in 1-2 days post pyrantel

### Monitoring
- 2-week pyrantel repeat dose calendar reminder
- Symptom recheck at 4 weeks
- If itching returns, consider albendazole 400 mg single + repeat (or extended pyrantel)
- Mark family calendar for quarterly pulse if recurrence pattern develops

## Case 3 — "Tier 3 Chronic Lyme + Mold + Suspected Babesia"

### Intake highlights
- 45F, severe fatigue, air hunger, drenching night sweats, brain fog, 5 yr history
- Multiple tick bites in NE US
- ELISA + Western blot Lyme positive
- Mold biotoxin panel positive (ochratoxin elevated)
- Babesia FISH (IGeneX) positive for B. microti
- Eosinophils 750, IgE 480
- Stool GI-MAP: Blastocystis subtype 3, Candida overgrowth, low SIgA
- Diet sensitivities, MCAS-like flares with food
- Currently moved out of moldy house 3 months ago
- Medications: low-dose naltrexone 4.5 mg, cetirizine 10 mg

### Tier
3 — multi-system, complex co-infection picture

### Decision
Extended Phase 1 (8 weeks) + ILADS-style Lyme/Babesia treatment + parasite layer + mold detox + mast cell management. Coordinate with prescribing clinician.

### Protocol

**Phase 1 (weeks 1-8) — extended**
- TUDCA 500 mg BID
- Phosphatidylcholine 1800 mg/day
- Liposomal glutathione 500 mg BID
- NAC 600 mg BID
- Quercetin 1 g BID
- Vitamin C 2 g divided
- Magnesium 600 mg/day mixed forms
- LMNT 2-3/day
- Cholestyramine 4 g BID at home (or Welchol if not tolerated) for ochratoxin
- BioActive Carbon Foundation 2 caps BID
- Triphala 1 g PM
- Slow drainage build, sauna 3x/week
- Mast cell diet (low histamine)
- DAO with high-histamine meals

**Phase 2 (weeks 9-24) — long course**
- Atovaquone 750 mg q12h with fatty meal (fill whole window for at least 6 weeks)
- Azithromycin 500 mg/day x 5 days then 250 mg/day continuous
- Cryptolepis tincture 5 mL TID
- Sida acuta tincture 5 mL TID
- Houttuynia 1 g TID (Bartonella co-coverage)
- Andrographis 400 mg BID
- Japanese knotweed (resveratrol) 400 mg BID
- Artemisinin 200 mg BID, 3 days on / 11 days off, 4 cycles total
- Mimosa Pudica per loading, 4 caps BID by week 12 (Blastocystis + biofilm)
- Berberine 500 mg BID weeks 12-18 for Blastocystis
- Caprylic acid 2 g BID for Candida
- Lumbrokinase 20 mg empty stomach BID for biofilm + microcirculation
- S. boulardii 10 billion BID throughout

**Phase 3 (concurrent + 4 wks post)**
- BAC Foundation, BAC ATP, Carboxy alternating
- Activated charcoal + bentonite at bedtime
- Cholestyramine continued through Phase 2
- Daily binders separated from atovaquone by 2 hr (atovaquone binds to charcoal)

**Phase 4 (weeks 25-40)**
- L-glutamine 10 g
- Zinc carnosine 75 mg BID
- Colostrum 6 g
- Probiotic rotation, low histamine strains
- PHGG building
- Bone broth, glycine 5 g PM
- CoQ10 200 mg, ALCAR 1 g, ribose 5 g BID, PQQ 20 mg for mitochondrial recovery
- Consider methylene blue 0.5 mg/kg under specialist if energy not recovering
- Adrenal cortex support, ashwagandha
- Sleep, sauna, sunlight, slow movement

### Monitoring
- Weekly symptom log
- CBC, CMP, LFT, retic, haptoglobin, LDH every 4 weeks while on atovaquone/azithromycin
- Babesia FISH/PCR repeat at week 16 and week 24
- Lyme symptom severity scale weekly
- Mast cell flare diary
- Mold mycotoxin retest at month 6

### Notes for Atlas
- LDN can stay; may want to titrate up to 4.5 → 5 → 6 mg over treatment as immune calms
- Cetirizine continued through MCAS recovery
- Coordinate with ILADS/Lyme physician for atovaquone Rx and methylene blue
- Pregnancy — confirm not planning during treatment

## Case 4 — "Empiric Family Maintenance"

### Intake highlights
- Derek's family — quarterly preventive cleanse
- All adults, healthy, asymptomatic
- Annual stool PCR negative
- Travel-prone, regular outdoor life
- 2 dogs, regularly dewormed

### Tier
1 — preventive

### Decision
1-week pulse at full moon, twice yearly (spring and fall), or quarterly if travel-heavy.

### Protocol (each pulse, all adults)

**Week of full moon**
- Mimosa Pudica 2 caps BID
- Black walnut tincture 1 mL BID
- Wormwood 250 mg BID (1 week only)
- Cloves 500 mg BID
- Garlic 600 mg/day or food dose
- Pumpkin seeds 2 tbsp/day in smoothie
- Phase 1 supports: TUDCA 250 mg/day, magnesium PM, electrolytes, hydration
- Binders 2x/day during week
- Saunas 3x in the week
- No alcohol, low sugar week
- Pets dewormed quarterly via vet

### Monitoring
- Symptom diary if any develop
- Annual stool PCR for adults
- Repeat pulse next quarter

## Case 5 — "Adult with Confirmed Strongyloides via Pre-Biologic Screen"

### Intake highlights
- 52M, scheduled to start anti-TNF for psoriatic arthritis
- Vietnam veteran with rural exposure
- Strongyloides IgG positive at 4.0 (cutoff 1.7)
- Asymptomatic
- Routine bloodwork unremarkable, eosinophils 480

### Tier
2 — asymptomatic but pre-immunosuppression mandates eradication

### Decision
Confirmed Strongyloides pathway + critical-importance language: eradicate before any immunosuppression.

### Protocol

**Phase 1 (weeks 1-2) — abbreviated due to time pressure**
- TUDCA 250 mg BID
- Magnesium 400 mg PM
- Hydration, electrolytes
- Mimosa Pudica loading

**Phase 2**
- **Ivermectin 200 mcg/kg PO daily x 2 days at week 2**
- **Repeat ivermectin 200 mcg/kg PO daily x 2 days at week 4**
- Mimosa Pudica 4 caps BID continued for 6+ months total
- Black walnut tincture 2 mL BID
- Optional albendazole 400 mg BID x 7 days at week 6 if heavy clinical suspicion

**Phase 3 / 4**
- Standard binders, gut restoration, microbiome rebuild
- Continue throughout 6-month mimosa course

### Monitoring
- Strongyloides IgG repeat at 6 months and 12 months — should decline
- CBC for eosinophil normalization
- Anti-TNF can begin 4-6 weeks after second ivermectin dose if asymptomatic
- Chart EHR alert: "Strongyloides positive 2026 — life-long; verify clear before any high-dose steroids"

## How Atlas Uses These Cases

When a new intake comes in, Atlas matches the closest case pattern, adapts the protocol to the patient's specifics, and checks differences:
- Tier match
- Comorbidity differences
- Medication conflicts
- Pediatric / pregnancy / IC
- Compliance / cost capacity
- Household / pet adjustments

If no case is a clean match, Atlas builds from decision tree first principles and flags the case for Derek's review.
